# Handy Pioneers — Design Brainstorm

## Context
Single-page conversion funnel for a Vancouver WA handyman & remodeling contractor. Two primary CTAs: "Book Online" (Housecall Pro widget) and "Log In to Portal." Must feel trustworthy, local, and professional — not corporate or generic.

## Idea 1: Pacific Northwest Craftsman

**Design Movement:** American Craftsman / Arts & Crafts Revival

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth through natural materials — wood grain textures, earthy tones
2. Structural honesty — visible grid lines, bold section dividers, no decorative excess
3. Handcrafted typography — serif display font paired with a clean sans-serif body
4. Trust through tangibility — real photos, real names, real neighborhoods

**Color Philosophy:** Deep forest green (#2D4A3E) as the primary, warm amber (#C8892A) as the accent (evoking wood and brass hardware), off-white (#F5F0E8) as the background. This palette communicates reliability and Pacific Northwest character without feeling corporate.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column layouts within sections. Hero is full-bleed with a left-aligned text block over a dark overlay. Services use a staggered card grid. Testimonials are horizontal scrolling cards with a wood-grain background strip.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin horizontal rule lines with small diamond or nail-head motifs as section dividers
2. A subtle wood-grain texture overlay on dark sections
3. Amber-colored CTA buttons with a slight bevel/shadow effect

**Interaction Philosophy:** Deliberate and confident. Hover states reveal a subtle amber underline on links. Buttons have a satisfying press-down effect. Scroll-triggered fade-ins for each section.

**Animation:** Sections fade and slide up gently on scroll. The hero headline types in letter by letter. CTA buttons have a warm glow pulse on idle.

**Typography System:** Display: `Playfair Display` (bold, serif) for H1/H2. Body: `Source Sans 3` (regular/semibold). CTA labels: `Source Sans 3` uppercase, tracked wide.

## Idea 2: Industrial Modern — Built to Last

**Design Movement:** Industrial Modernism / Brutalist-lite

**Core Principles:**
1. Bold, unapologetic typography — oversized section labels, high contrast
2. Dark anchoring — a near-black charcoal base with bright accent pops
3. Grid discipline — strict column alignment, deliberate negative space
4. Proof over promise — stats, credentials, and photos dominate over copy

**Color Philosophy:** Charcoal (#1C1C1E) as the dominant background for hero and CTA sections, warm white (#FAFAF8) for content sections, and a vivid construction-orange (#E8621A) as the single accent color. Orange is energetic, trustworthy in trades, and highly visible on both dark and light backgrounds.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width alternating dark/light sections. Hero is a split layout: left half is a dark panel with text and CTAs, right half is a full-bleed photo. Services are displayed as a numbered list with large numerals, not a card grid.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Large, outlined numerals (01, 02, 03...) as section anchors
2. A thick orange left-border accent on testimonial quotes
3. High-contrast "BOOK ONLINE" button in solid orange with white text

**Interaction Philosophy:** Fast and direct. No unnecessary animations. Hover states are immediate color inversions. The booking button has a subtle shake animation on first load to draw attention.

**Animation:** Minimal. Counter animations for stats (e.g., "18 Services," "5-Star Rating"). Smooth scroll. No parallax.

**Typography System:** Display: `Barlow Condensed` (extra-bold, condensed) for H1/H2 — gives a strong, structural feel. Body: `Barlow` (regular). A very different feel from Inter.

## Idea 3: Clean Pacific Pro — Trustworthy & Conversion-Focused

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Local Business / Conversion-Optimized

**Core Principles:**
1. Clarity above all — every element serves the conversion goal
2. Warm professionalism — not cold corporate, not rustic either
3. Generous whitespace with strong visual anchors
4. Mobile-first layout that works perfectly on a phone screen

**Color Philosophy:** A deep slate blue (#1E3A5F) as the primary — communicating reliability and professionalism. A warm amber-gold (#D4882A) as the accent — echoing the existing brand's brown/gold buttons. Clean white (#FFFFFF) background for content sections. This palette is familiar enough to retain brand recognition while feeling more polished.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with a strong left-aligned headline. Below the fold, sections alternate between full-width and contained widths. Services use a clean 3-column icon grid. Testimonials are stacked cards with a subtle shadow.

**Signature Elements:**
1. A sticky top bar with the phone number and "Log In to Portal" button
2. A floating "Book Online" button that appears after scrolling past the hero
3. A final CTA section with a dark background and centered booking button

**Interaction Philosophy:** Frictionless. Every CTA is immediately visible. The booking widget opens smoothly. Smooth scroll between anchor sections.

**Animation:** Subtle fade-in on scroll for each section. A gentle pulse on the primary CTA button. Smooth transitions on hover states.

**Typography System:** Display: `Oswald` (bold, condensed) for H1/H2 — strong and readable. Body: `Open Sans` (regular/semibold). Clean, legible, and widely trusted.

## Selected Approach: Idea 1 — Pacific Northwest Craftsman

This approach best reflects the Handy Pioneers brand identity (existing brown/gold color scheme, craftsman logo) while elevating it to a more premium, hand-crafted feel. The warm, earthy palette and serif typography communicate trustworthiness and local character — exactly what a homeowner in Clark County, WA wants to see when choosing a contractor.
