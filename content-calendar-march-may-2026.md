---

### Strategic Framework

Every post is built on three layers:

**Layer 1 — Hook, Story, Offer (HSO)**
Each post opens with a pattern-interrupt hook (a surprising fact, local stat, or bold claim), transitions into a real story (a completed job, a homeowner's situation, a trend playing out in Clark County), and closes with a soft offer (a free estimate CTA, a downloadable checklist, or a link to book). The framework is never named in the post — it simply shapes the writing.

**Layer 2 — The 360° Method (introduced gradually)**
Starting in Week 3, posts begin referencing the 360° Method by name — first as a philosophy ("we call it our 360° approach"), then as a named system ("the 360° Method"), and by May as a framework with named pillars. This seeds the brand before the full app/education launch. Posts never explain the full system — they create curiosity and authority.

**Layer 3 — Audience Rotation**
Posts rotate across six audience segments so every reader type sees content made for them at least once every two weeks.

| Audience Code | Segment |
|---|---|
| **HC** | Homeowners — Current Clients |
| **HP** | Homeowners — Prospects |
| **EM** | Employees & Technicians |
| **SC** | Subcontractors & Trade Partners |
| **PT** | Business Partners & Referral Sources |
| **MK** | Local Market / Community (Clark County) |

## Implementation Notes

### Publishing Workflow
Each post should follow this production sequence: (1) write draft using the title, hook, story, and offer above as the brief, (2) add 1–3 real photos from the job or relevant stock, (3) add internal links to the related project detail page where noted, (4) publish to the site's blog section, (5) share to Google Business Profile as a post, (6) share to Instagram and Facebook with a shortened hook as the caption.

### Internal Linking Strategy
Every post that references a completed project should link to the corresponding `/project/:slug` detail page. This creates a content cluster that reinforces both the blog post and the project page in Google's index.
